Chicken Nuggets

Amazing chicken nuggets

Learn how to make the best chicken nuggets right out of the freezer!

Ingredients

  • Frozen Chicken nuggets

Steps

  1. Take bag of chicken nuggets out of freezer
  2. Turn on air fryer to 400 degrees Farenheit
  3. Lay out nuggets in air fryer in a single layer
  4. Cook for 10 minutes or until desired crispiness
  5. Take nuggets out of the air fryer and enjoy!
