Lasagna

Delicious meat lasagna

Learn how to make the best meat lasagna you've ever had in less than an hour!

Ingredients

  • No bake lasagna noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Put sauce in your baking dish
  2. Lay noodles evenly along bottom of pan
  3. Add sauce on top of noodles
  4. Add Ricotta in dollops along the noodles and sauce
  5. Add a handful of Mozzarella and Parmesean cheese across top
  6. Repeat these steps until you reach the top of the baking dish, or how thick yopu would like your lasagna
  7. Finish off the top with a healthy layer of Mozzarella and parmesean
  8. Cover lasagna with tin foil
  9. Bake in oven for 45 minutes
