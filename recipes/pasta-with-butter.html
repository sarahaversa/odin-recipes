Pasta with butter and cheese

Learn to make the best and easiest pasta ever!

Ingredients

  • Pasta shape of your choice
  • Butter
  • Parmesean Cheese(optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot on the stove
  2. Add salt to boiling water
  3. Add pasta to boiling water
  4. Cook pasta to packet instructions
  5. Drain pasta water and put pasta back in pot
  6. Add butter to pot with pasta. Stir until melted.
  7. (Optional)Add cheese if desiered
  8. Enjoy!
